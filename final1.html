i am from that place where mountains meet sky,

where the sunshine feels like warm honey,

como agua para chocolate, like water for chocolate,

como sangre para lágrimas. like blood for tears.

ten piedad. have mercy.

i am from beans and chile,

where our streets are burro trails,

and sometimes it feels like the sky might drink you up.

the smell of thunderstorms,

family crammed around the table,

and jalapeños from the garden,

that's where i'm from.
