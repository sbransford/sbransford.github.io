i am from cowboys and indians,

from curanderas and conquistadores, from medicine women and spanish conquerors,

como risa para medicamento, like laughter for medicine,

un abrazo para sanar, a hug to heal,

como tus labios para la verdad. like your lips for the truth.

and i know i can never go too far,

where i can't come back home again,

i feel their hands on my back,

guiding me,

pushing me,

con piedad.

de hecho somos de la misma sol. in fact we are from the same sun.

thank you for reading!
