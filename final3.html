i am from red caliche and blue atole,

where the people's skin is as brown as the earth,

from, “¿tienes hambre, hita?” from, "are you hungry, my child?"

and, "if you have time to lean, you have time to clean."

i am from trabajadoras, i am from hard-working women,

que les encanta bailar, that love to dance,

and are always willing to do what needs to be done.

i am from crisp starry nights,

and coyote songs,

where the people will give you the food from their plate,

the shirt from their back,

if only you'd ask.

ten piedad.
